Phenotype Prediction Analysis

Purpose

We analyze phenotype predictions made by LLMs for bacterial species. This page shows:

  1. Number of predictions each model made
  2. Distribution of predicted values for each phenotype characteristic
  3. Frequency of NA/Failed predictions per model and phenotype

Key Insight: Models that provide specific predictions (rather than NA) while maintaining accuracy demonstrate better understanding of microbial phenotypes.

How to Interpret Results

Phenotype Categories:

Gram Staining positive, negative, variable
Motility TRUE, FALSE
Aerophilicity aerobic, anaerobic, facultative, aerotolerant
Pathogenicity animal, plant, human health
What to look for: Models with fewer NA responses and more specific predictions generally indicate better training and understanding. However, accuracy of these predictions is also crucial.
