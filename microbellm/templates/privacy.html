This privacy policy provides an overview of how we handle your personal data when you visit our website. We explain what data we collect, how we process it, and for what purposes.

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

5. Plugins and Tools

Matomo Analytics

We use the Matomo Cloud service operated by InnoCraft Ltd (Matomo) to measure how visitors use the site. The service processes technical usage information such as the pages viewed, referrers, truncated IP addresses, device and browser details, and interaction timestamps. Matomo only starts collecting data after you provide consent via the analytics banner that appears on your first visit.

The legal basis for this processing is your consent pursuant to Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR. When you allow analytics, Matomo stores first-party cookies to recognise returning visits and aggregates the resulting metrics for us. Data is hosted in the Matomo Cloud infrastructure managed by Helmholtz-Zentrum für Infektionsforschung GmbH.

You can change your decision at any time by opening the consent manager. Click here to reopen the consent settings and choose “Decline” to revoke consent. Klaro will update your choice immediately and Matomo stops sending analytics events as soon as consent is withdrawn.

Further information about Matomo's data processing is available in the Matomo privacy policy.

Font Awesome

This website uses Font Awesome for font representation. Font Awesome may process certain technical data according to their privacy policy. For more information about Font Awesome's data processing, please refer to their privacy policy.
